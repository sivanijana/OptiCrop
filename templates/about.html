About OptiCrop

A next-generation crop recommendation platform that helps farmers and agri-businesses make precision-driven decisions.

Project Overview

OptiCrop uses machine learning to analyze soil and weather characteristics and recommend the most suitable crop for a given farm context.

Objectives

  • Improve crop selection accuracy
  • Reduce input waste and improve sustainability
  • Deliver insights through a premium digital experience

Machine Learning Algorithms

The platform compares Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and KNN models to identify the most accurate predictive approach.

Benefits

Higher productivity, lower risk, optimized fertilizer usage, and improved decision support for growers and advisors.
